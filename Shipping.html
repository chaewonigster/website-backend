Shipping + Delivery FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our shipping and delivery services.

Shipping + Delivery

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer three convenient delivery options: Local delivery, Lalamove delivery service, or you can choose to pick up your order from our location. Each option is designed to provide you with flexibility based on your needs.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we do not ship internationally. We're focused on providing the best service to our local customers while maintaining the quality of our frozen products. We may expand our shipping options in the future.

Why have I only received part of my order?

Your order may have been split into multiple shipments due to product availability or to ensure optimal freshness of certain items. Please check your order confirmation email for tracking information on all packages. If you have concerns about missing items, please contact our customer service team.

I received a notice my package has arrived and it's not here yet, what should I do?

First, please check with your local delivery service or neighbors to see if the package was delivered elsewhere. If you still cannot locate your package, contact our support team with your order number and delivery details, and we'll help track down your order right away.

My order arrived damaged. What should I do?

We're sorry to hear that your order arrived damaged! Please take photos of the damaged items and packaging, then contact our customer support team immediately with your order number and the photos. We'll process a replacement or refund as quickly as possible to resolve the issue.

How long does shipping usually take?

For local delivery, orders are typically delivered within 24-48 hours. Lalamove deliveries depend on their availability and your location but usually arrive same-day or next-day. For pickup orders, you can collect your items as soon as we notify you that they're ready, typically within a few hours of placing your order.

Are there any shipping fees?

Yes, shipping fees vary based on your delivery method and location. Local delivery has a base fee with additional charges for longer distances. Lalamove fees are calculated based on their current rates. Pickup orders do not incur shipping fees. The exact cost will be calculated and displayed during checkout before you complete your purchase.
