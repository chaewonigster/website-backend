Shipping + Delivery

What shipping methods do you offer?

Local delivery, Lalamove or Pickup

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we do not ship internationally.

Why have I only received part of my order?

Your order may have been split into multiple shipments. Please check your tracking information for details.

I received a notice my package has arrived and it's not here yet, what should I do?

Please check with your local delivery service or your neighbors to see if it was delivered elsewhere. If it still hasn't arrived, contact our support team.

My order arrived damaged. What should I do?

We’re sorry to hear that! Please contact our support team with your order details to resolve the issue.
